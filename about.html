Personal Projects

For my most recent personal project, I wanted to create a visualization related to one of my favorite hobbies, the Rocket League esports scene. Rocket League is a 3v3 team game simulating soccer, however, instead of playing as a human, you control a rocket-powered car. In the competitive scene, the top teams from each region are invited to international events periodically. As it stands, the current visualizations for team rankings by region make it difficult for users to see teamsâ€™ growth and failures over the course of the year long season. Noticing this opportunity, I conducted a short task and stakeholder analysis to create a rough draft of a visualization designed to fill this gap. Once satisfied with a bump chart design, I began scrapping the data online using the Python package Beautiful Soup. After scrapping the data, I used R and related tidyverse packages to clean and organize the data. With the cleaned data, I used JavaScript (D3.js) to create the bump chart showing the Rocket League Championship Series rankings for the 2021-2022 season. One interesting insight visualized with this chart, is the rise of Oxygen Esports in North America. Hovering over this team, we can see they start the season with 0 points ranked 24th but end the season with 1,110 points ranked 8th in their region, showing the team became more competitive towards the latter half of the season.
