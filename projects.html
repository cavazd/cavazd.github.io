NOVA: An AI Powered NIST CSF Education and Management Tool (KPMG-Sponsored Capstone Project)

NOTE: The server for this app has been indefinitely paused due to the end of academic work on this project.
I played a key role in developing an interactive chatbot project focused on assisting users learn and navigate through NIST CSF (National Institute of Standards and Technology Cybersecurity Framework) documentation. Leveraging Python and the OpenAI API, I collected, cleaned, and encoded relevant NIST CSF documents to create a robust training corpus. Additionally, I authored informative and comprehensive documentation, including user guides and docstrings, to ensure a seamless handoff of the project to our sponsor.
